Use this after a candidate accepts an offer and you need them ready for training, credentials, and deployment.

### Before you start

- The application should be in an **offer accepted** or equivalent stage.
- Gather ID, licensing, and emergency contact information your organization requires.

### Steps

1. Open the hired candidate's **application** in **Hiring → Applications**.
2. Complete any remaining **checks** or document uploads required by policy.
3. Convert or link the record to a **People** employee profile (wording depends on your tenant setup).
4. On the employee profile, confirm **job title**, **start date**, and **work location** fields.
5. Upload credential documents (licenses, certifications) to **Documents** if applicable.
6. Assign required **policies** for acknowledgement.
7. Open **Operations roster** and verify the employee link status if your organization deploys through Operations.
8. Notify training (Watchman **Launch**) if the hire must complete academy courses before deployment.

### After onboarding

- Track time-off balances and compensation changes only through authorized HR roles.
- Use **Reports** for leadership visibility once the record is complete.

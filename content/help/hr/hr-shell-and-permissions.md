Watchman HR is built so people only see the **menus and actions** that match their job. Recruiters, HR managers, payroll partners, and executives may each have different access.

### Day-to-day HR work

Most users spend time in **People**, **Hiring**, **Compensation**, **Time off**, **Policies**, and **Documents**—whatever your organization enabled for their role.

### Sensitive actions

A smaller group may manage **approvals**, **disciplinary** records, **offboarding**, or **settings** such as communications templates and integrations. Everyday hiring coordinators do not need that level of access.

### If you need more access

Ask your **HR manager** or **Watchman administrator**. Access changes should follow your internal approval process and tenant membership rules.

Watchman HR includes an **Operations roster** view that shows how employee records map to field deployment context.

### Why it matters

Security organizations need hiring, credentials, and **who is deployable** to stay aligned. The roster link reduces gaps between HR records and Operations staffing.

### Typical workflow

1. Complete hiring and onboarding in **Hiring** and **People**.
2. Confirm credentials and readiness on the employee profile.
3. Open **Operations roster** to verify the Operations link status.
4. Use **Sync** or integration tools (when enabled) to align staff with Operations.

### Unlinked employees

If someone appears in People but not on the Operations side, filter for **unlinked** records from Operations roster or People and resolve via your administrator or integrations settings under **Settings → Integrations**.

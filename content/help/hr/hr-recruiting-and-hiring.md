Watchman HR centers hiring under **Hiring** in the sidebar. The main areas are:

- **Pipeline** — stages from new applicant through offer and hire
- **Applications** — individual applicant records, references, and checks
- **Postings** — published careers roles (may also surface on Launch-powered careers pages)

### How hiring connects to the suite

- **Launch** can publish careers and accept applications that flow into HR.
- **Operations** receives deployment context once a hire is linked on the **Operations roster**.
- **Finance** can receive compensation intent when payroll integrations are enabled.

### Good practices

- Keep one application record per candidate; use pipeline stages instead of duplicate entries.
- Close or archive postings when a role is filled.
- Document offer decisions in the application timeline for auditability.

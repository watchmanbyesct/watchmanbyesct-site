The **People** area is the system of record for workforce context in Watchman HR.

### What lives on a person record

- Employment status, role, and organizational placement
- Documents and policy acknowledgements
- Compensation and time-off context (when modules are enabled)
- Links to **Operations** deployment when roster sync is configured

### Letters and outbound HR communications

HR can generate letters (for example offer or employment correspondence) using tenant letterhead when branding is configured in Operations settings.

### Exports and reporting

Authorized roles can export directory data and use **Reports** for leadership views. Exports should follow your organization's data-handling policy.

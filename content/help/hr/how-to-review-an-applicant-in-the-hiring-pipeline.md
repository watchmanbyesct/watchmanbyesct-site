Use this when a new application arrives and you need to move it through your organization's hiring stages.

### Before you start

- Confirm you have access to **Hiring** in Watchman HR.
- Know your organization's stage names (for example *Screening*, *Interview*, *Offer*).

### Steps

1. Sign in to **Watchman HR** and open **Hiring** from the sidebar.
2. Open **Applications** (or start from **Pipeline** if you work by stage).
3. Select the applicant you need to review.
4. Read the application details, resume attachments, and any screening answers.
5. Check **references** or background items if your workflow requires them.
6. Add an internal **note** if you need to record a phone screen or manager feedback.
7. Move the application to the next **pipeline stage**, or mark it **declined** with a reason if your process requires one.
8. If the candidate is ready to hire, follow your internal steps to generate an **offer** (see your HR manager's offer workflow).

### If something fails

- **Missing posting:** Confirm the careers posting is still published if the candidate applied externally.
- **Cannot change stage:** Your role may be read-only—ask an HR manager to adjust access.

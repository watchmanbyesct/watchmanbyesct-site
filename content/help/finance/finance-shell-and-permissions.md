Watchman Finance is built so people only see the **menus and actions** that match their job. Payroll clerks, AP staff, controllers, and auditors may each have different access.

### Day-to-day finance work

Most users spend time in **invoices**, **bills**, **payroll**, **banking**, and **reports**—whatever your organization enabled for their role.

### Platform administration

A small group at your company (or at Watchman / ESCT for hosted setups) may have extra screens to **add users**, **reset access**, or **turn major areas on or off**. Everyday bookkeepers do not need that level of access.

### If you need more access

Ask your **finance manager** or **Watchman administrator**. Access changes should follow your internal approval process.

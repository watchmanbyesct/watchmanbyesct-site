Watchman Finance can connect to **supported accounting packages** so your team spends less time retyping data.

### Before you connect

- Use a **company officer** or **bookkeeper** account that is allowed to authorize apps for your business.
- Have your accounting **sign-in** ready; you may be sent to the provider’s website to approve access.

### Typical flow

1. In Finance, open **Integrations** or **Accounting connection** (wording may vary).
2. Choose your **provider** and start the connection.
3. Sign in at the provider when prompted and **approve** the access Watchman requests.
4. Return to Watchman and confirm the screen shows **Connected**.
5. If the connection drops later, repeat the flow or contact support—providers sometimes expire logins for security.

### If something fails

Check that you used the **correct company file** at the provider. If your organization uses an accountant who manages the books, they may need to perform the connection for you.

**Period close** steps differ by company; this article lists a sensible default order. Follow your CFO or controller’s checklist if it conflicts.

### Before you start

- Confirm **posting period** dates for the month you are closing.
- Ensure **integrations** have finished for payroll and operational billing feeds.

### Steps

1. **Banking:** Complete **reconciliations** for all operating accounts through the statement end date.
2. **AR:** Post all **cash receipts** for the month; review **aging** for material open items.
3. **AP:** Enter **bills** received before month-end; schedule or record **outbound payments** that belong in the period.
4. **Payroll:** Finalize **pay runs** whose pay dates fall in the month; post payroll journals.
5. **General ledger:** Review **suspense** or **uncategorized** accounts and clear or reclassify entries with your controller’s approval.
6. **Tax / statutory:** Run any required **liability** or **deposit** accruals for the month if your company uses those areas of Watchman.
7. When satisfied, use **close period** or **lock period** (if enabled) so new postings cannot backdate into a closed month without override.

### Controls

- Require a **second reviewer** for period lock if your policy demands segregation of duties.
- Attach a **close memo** or checklist PDF to your evidence store if your company tracks close packages in Watchman.

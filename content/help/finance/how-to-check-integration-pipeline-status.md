Finance shows whether information from **Operations** and **Launch**—such as approved hours and billing-related records—has arrived and processed successfully.

### Before you start

- You need access to the **Integrations** (or similar) area in Watchman Finance.
- Know whether you are looking at **live** data or a **test** environment so you do not misread results.

### Steps

1. Sign in to **Watchman Finance** and open **Integrations** or **Integration pipeline** from the menu.
2. Note the **date and time** of the last successful run and the overall **status** (for example all clear, warnings, or errors).
3. If you see an **error**, open the details for that line and read the short **message** shown on screen.
4. Write down the **date**, **time**, and message text—your operations lead or Watchman support will ask for that when helping you.
5. If the message points to missing information in **Operations** or **Launch**, work with the person who owns that data to correct it, then check Finance again after the next scheduled update (or use **Run again** / **Refresh** if your screen offers it).
6. If the problem persists, contact **Watchman support** with the notes you collected.

### Tips

- Check after known maintenance windows before opening a ticket.
- A screenshot of the status screen often speeds up support.

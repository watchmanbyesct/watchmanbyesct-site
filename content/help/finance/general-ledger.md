The **GL** is the accounting backbone: entities, chart of accounts, periods, postings, and journal lifecycle.

### Why it matters

AR, AP, payroll, tax, and reporting integrity ultimately rest on disciplined GL operations.

### For operators

Server actions enforce permissions and audit logging; pages are data-dense and desktop-first.

Use the public training area of your organization’s Launch-powered site to enroll. Exact labels may match your academy’s wording (for example **Training** or **Courses**).

### Before you start

- You need the **correct website URL** for your training provider.
- Have a working **email address** and phone number ready; confirmations are sent by email.

### Steps

1. Open the organization’s **training** or **academy** section from the main navigation.
2. Browse or search for the **course** you need (for example DCJS-required modules).
3. Open the **session** that fits your schedule and read prerequisites, location, and fees.
4. Tap **Enroll**, **Register**, or **Sign up** (wording varies by site).
5. Complete the **enrollment form** (name, contact, employer if required, acknowledgements).
6. If payment is required before confirmation, you will be sent to **checkout** next—see [How to complete training checkout](/help/launch/how-to-complete-training-checkout).
7. After submission, check your **email** for confirmation and any instructions to access the **student portal**.

### If something fails

- **Session full:** Pick another session or join a waitlist if offered.
- **No confirmation email:** Check spam, then contact the academy using the contact link on the same site.

### For operators

Enrollment writes to your Launch training tables; admins manage sessions and capacity in the admin dashboard.

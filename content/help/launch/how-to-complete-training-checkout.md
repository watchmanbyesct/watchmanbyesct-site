Checkout uses **Stripe** behind the scenes. You pay on a secure page hosted for your training provider.

### Before you start

- A valid **debit or credit card** (or whatever methods your provider enabled).
- Enrollment flow already started—checkout usually appears **after** you fill the registration form.

### Steps

1. From the enrollment confirmation step, open **Pay now** or **Continue to payment**.
2. On the checkout page, enter **card details** and billing information as prompted.
3. Submit payment and wait for the **success** message—do not close the tab until it completes.
4. When verification succeeds, Launch marks the enrollment **paid** (or equivalent status) and eligibility for attendance/certificates updates automatically.
5. Return to the **confirmation** or **student dashboard** link from the success screen or email.

### If payment fails

- Retry with the same card after confirming your bank did not block the charge.
- Use a different card if your provider allows it.
- Contact the academy if the site shows **paid** but you did not receive email—the office can correct your enrollment status on their side.

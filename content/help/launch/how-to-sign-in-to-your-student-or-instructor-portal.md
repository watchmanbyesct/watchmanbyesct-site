Launch provides **role-based portals** (student, instructor, member, and internal roles). You always sign in from the same organization website.

### Before you start

- Know whether you are logging in as a **student**, **instructor**, or another role—your dashboard matches that role.
- Use the **email** your organization has on file (often the one used at enrollment).

### Steps

1. Open the organization’s **Launch** site (bookmark the URL your provider gave you).
2. Choose **Sign in**, **Log in**, or **Portal** from the header or footer.
3. Enter **email** and **password**. If you never set a password, use **Forgot password** to receive a reset link.
4. After sign-in, you land on the **dashboard** for your role.
5. **Students:** open **My courses**, **Schedule**, or **Certificates** from the dashboard or menu.
6. **Instructors:** open **Classes**, **Attendance**, or **Rosters** depending on what your academy enabled.

### If you cannot sign in

- Confirm you are on the **correct organization URL** (not a generic marketing page).
- Reset password once, then wait a few minutes before trying again.
- Ask the academy admin to confirm your **account exists** and the **role** is correct.

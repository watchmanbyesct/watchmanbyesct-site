Build pipelines can enforce **contract and synthetic checks**, and scheduled workflows may probe production endpoints on a cadence (for example every **15 minutes**).

### Value

Earlier detection of outages or regressions across critical journeys.

### For operators

This is operational telemetry—not a student-facing feature. Secrets must be present for probes to be meaningful.

Incidents are formal records—often reviewed by supervisors, compliance, and clients. Accuracy and timeliness matter.

### Before you start

- Ensure you are **safe** before typing; escalate emergencies per post orders first.
- Photos should respect **privacy** and **site rules** (no restricted areas).

### Steps

1. Open **Incidents** in the guard or supervisor shell.
2. Choose **New incident** and select the **site** and **time** closest to the event.
3. Choose **severity** and **category** from the options your tenant configured.
4. Enter a **clear title** and **description** (who, what, when, where, outcome, notifications made).
5. Add **attachments** (images, PDFs) and **GPS** context if the form requests them.
6. **Submit** the report. If your workflow uses **escalation**, confirm alerts fired or follow SOP to call dispatch.
7. To **update** an open incident, open it from the list, add a **follow-up note** or new attachment, and save—do not overwrite original facts; add clarifying notes instead.

### After submission

- Watch for **supervisor feedback** or requests for more detail in the app or email.
- Preserve any **physical evidence** per company policy.

### For operators

Incident feeds and **client portal** visibility depend on tenant modules and publication rules.

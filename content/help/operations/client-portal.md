The **client portal** gives your customers a dedicated sign-in experience for transparency: overview, incidents, reports, requests, and communications context—**only** what your company chooses to share.

### Expectations

What clients see depends on **what your team publishes** and what is included in your service agreement. Day-to-day guard and supervisor screens stay internal unless you release information to the portal.

### Common visibility matrix

- **Overview** is usually visible.
- **Incidents** are commonly visible.
- **Reports** and **Requests** are often contract/configuration dependent.
- Communications and service context may be exposed by provider policy.

The **supervisor desktop** is the primary control surface for running a security program: incidents, dispatch, scheduling, timekeeping, DAR, patrol and checkpoints, post orders, handover, sites, personnel, and communications (when enabled).

### Display

The layout is **desktop-first** with a grouped sidebar. On a **tablet or small laptop**, the menu usually tucks into a **drawer** you open from the edge of the screen.

Administrators can review **integration health** signals across Watchman products: connectivity, sync metrics, and provider state.

### Why it matters

Degraded integrations can affect staffing updates, invoicing, report freshness, or downstream financial pipelines.

### For operators

The UI calls health APIs; usefulness depends on secrets and configuration being complete in each environment.

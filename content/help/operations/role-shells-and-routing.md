Watchman Operations opens the right **workspace** for you based on your role and what your organization has turned on for your account.

### What you will see

- **Supervisor desktop** — A command-center style layout with a side menu for scheduling, incidents, reports, and administration.
- **Guard field app** — A phone-friendly app with bottom navigation for timekeeping, daily reports, patrol, incidents, and safety tools.
- **Client portal** — A separate sign-in experience for customers who are invited to see service information (only if your contract includes it).
- **Supervisor mobile** — A compact phone layout for supervisors who need incidents, dispatch, and review tools while away from the desk.

### If a screen or menu is missing

Menus and pages only appear for roles that are allowed to use them. Your company may also hide whole areas until you are trained. If you think you should have access, ask your **supervisor** or your company’s **Watchman administrator** to check your role and assignments.

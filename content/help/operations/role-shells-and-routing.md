Watchman Operations opens the right **workspace** for you based on your role and what your organization has turned on for your account.

### What you will see

- **Supervisor desktop** (`/`) — Command-center style layout with operations, workforce, field, intelligence, and management modules.
- **Guard field app** (`/guard`) — Phone-friendly field shell for timekeeping, DAR, incidents, patrol/checkpoints, handover, and safety tools.
- **Client portal** — A separate sign-in experience for customers who are invited to see service information (only if your contract includes it).
- **Supervisor mobile** (`/supervisor-mobile`) — Compact phone layout with both field and desk operations, including guard status, incidents, dispatch, scheduling, and DAR review.
- **Kiosk clock** (`/kiosk`) — Shared-tablet clock flow that uses guard ID + PIN instead of full personal app login.

### Why menus differ by person

Watchman gates visibility by both:

- **Role** (guard, supervisor, client, platform owner)
- **Tenant modules** (for example dispatch, patrol, communications, intelligence, billing)

### If a screen or menu is missing

Menus and pages only appear for roles that are allowed to use them. Your company may also hide whole areas until you are trained. If you think you should have access, ask your **supervisor** or your company’s **Watchman administrator** to check your role and assignments.

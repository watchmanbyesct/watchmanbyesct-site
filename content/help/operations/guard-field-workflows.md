Guards run shifts from a **mobile-first** interface: bottom navigation, a shift drawer, and a prominent clock action for timekeeping.

### Typical guard workflow

1. Confirm assignment, site, and post orders.
2. Clock in and execute shift duties.
3. Run patrol/checkpoint scans and log observations.
4. Submit DAR and incidents as needed.
5. Complete handover and clock out.

### Feature areas

- **Shift execution:** dashboard, schedule, briefing, timekeeping, time off
- **Field tasks:** patrol/checkpoints, dispatch, maintenance, vehicle inspection
- **Reporting:** DAR and incidents
- **Safety/comms:** safety alerts, lone worker checks, team messages, push alerts

### What it produces

Field activity creates records that power supervisor dashboards, the report center, and (where enabled) client reporting.

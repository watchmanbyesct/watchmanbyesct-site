Supervisors can manage **field operations** from a dedicated **mobile layout**: guard status, incidents, dispatch, inspections (when your company uses them), DAR review, and whether the device is online.

### When to use it

Use it when you are away from the desktop but need command visibility—especially incident and dispatch follow-up.

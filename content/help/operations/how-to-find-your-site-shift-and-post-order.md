Guards and field supervisors use the **Operations** mobile experience to see where they are working and what rules apply.

### Before you start

- You must be **signed in** with a guard (or field) role.
- Your supervisor should have assigned you to a **shift** and **site**.

### Steps

1. Open the **guard** app shell (narrow mobile layout with bottom navigation).
2. Open **Dashboard** or **Assignments** (wording varies by tenant) to see **today’s shift**.
3. Tap your **site** or **post** to view **post orders**—read special instructions, contacts, and escalation rules.
4. If your tenant uses **maps** or **site documents**, open attachments linked from the same screen.
5. If something looks wrong (wrong site, missing post order), use **Messages** or your SOP to contact the **supervisor** before clocking in.

### Tips

- Scroll to the bottom of long post orders for **version** or **effective date** if shown.
- Acknowledge post orders when the app asks—this records that you saw the current version.

### For operators

Assignments and post orders are driven by scheduling and **post orders** modules; module visibility depends on tenant **module governance**.
